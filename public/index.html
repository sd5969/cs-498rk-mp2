About

We are working on controlling Environmental Pollution and preserving the natural environment is our prime objective. We started our River cleanup and restoration effort with the Yamuna River near New Delhi and Agra, India.

We'd love to have your support in this effort. The "Yamuna River Remediation Project," in New Delhi, India, was started single-handedly back in October 1992. Since then we have gained support from various organizations and individuals and have now formed a group of dedicated individuals with one goal, one mind, and one motto of one kind - the "Blue Yamuna".

Mission Statement

The primary objective of the Rivers of the World (ROW) Foundation is to make all efforts to cleanup highly polluted Rivers in US, India, SE Asia, S. America, Africa, Europe, and Other Parts of the World.

Some of our major tenets:

  1. Inform and involve the public in activities that foster the protection, enhancement, and establishment of clean rivers and sustainable development along the river banks and riparian areas.
  2. Identify, evaluate, and address threats to the biological, cultural, and economic components of conservation of the river and riparian areas.
  3. Encourage voluntary participation of all potential partners to enhance and restore the biological, economic, and cultural well being of the rivers and riparian areas through educational, technical, and financial involvement.
